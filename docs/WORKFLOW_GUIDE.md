# Clynr Workflow Guide

This guide explains how to accomplish common tasks in the Clynr app, organized by workflow.

---

## Client Management

### Creating a New Client

1. Go to **Clients** tab
2. Tap the **+** button
3. Enter client details:
   - **Option A**: Enter First Name + Last Name
   - **Option B**: Enter Company Name (auto-enables "Use company as primary")
4. Add phone number and email (optional)
5. The first site "Head Office" is created automatically
6. Fill in site address (use Google Places search for auto-complete)
7. Tap **Save**

### Adding Multiple Sites to a Client

1. Open client details (tap client in Clients tab)
2. Tap **Edit**
3. Scroll to Sites section
4. Tap **Add Site**
5. Enter site nickname and address
6. Set GPS mode (Automatic, Reminder, or Off)
7. Tap **Save**

---

## Job Management

### Creating a New Job

1. Go to **Jobs** tab
2. Tap the **+** button → **New Job**
3. Select a client
4. Choose site address
5. Select job type: **One-off** or **Recurring**
6. Add service types from your catalog
7. Set visit date and time
8. Assign an employee (optional)
9. Add visit instructions (optional)
10. Tap **Save**

### Viewing Job Details

1. Go to **Jobs** tab
2. Tap on any job card
3. View sections:
   - Job Info (client, address, services)
   - Visits (list of scheduled visits)
   - Job Total (pricing summary)
   - Job History (completed forms/checklists)

### Changing Job Status

1. Open job details
2. Tap the status badge in the header
3. Select new status:
   - Scheduled
   - In Progress
   - Completed
   - On Hold
   - Cancelled

### Generating an Invoice from a Job

1. Open job details
2. Scroll to **Job Total** section
3. Review line items and total
4. Tap **Generate Invoice**
5. Invoice is created as draft and linked to the job

## Request Management

Requests are enquiries from potential or existing clients that may be converted into jobs.

### Request Types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **New Client Enquiry** | New business enquiry requiring a site visit |
| **Existing Client Request** | Additional or one-off services for an existing client |
| **Consumables Order** | Client ordering products |

### Request Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **New** | Just received, not yet reviewed |
| **Pending** | Being reviewed or awaiting more info |
| **Accepted** | Approved, ready to schedule |
| **Rejected** | Declined |
| **Converted** | Converted to a job |
| **Visit Complete** | Site visit has been completed |
| **Archived** | No longer active |

### Creating a New Request

1. Go to **Jobs** tab
2. Tap the **+** button
3. Select **New Request**
4. Choose request type:
   - New Client Enquiry
   - Existing Client Request
   - Consumables Order
5. Fill in required fields:
   - **Title** (required)
   - **Services Needed** (select from catalog)
   - **Client** (select existing or enter new contact info)
   - **Site/Location** (for site visits)
6. Add optional details:
   - Preferred date
   - Additional notes
   - Photos or videos
7. For site visits, schedule visit details:
   - Visit date and time
   - Assigned team member
   - Reminder settings
8. Tap **Save**

### Viewing Requests

1. Go to **More** tab
2. Tap **Requests** (or access via Jobs FAB menu)
3. Use filters to view:
   - **Schedule filters**: All, Today, Overdue, Upcoming, Unscheduled
   - **Status filters**: New, Pending, Accepted, etc.
4. Tap any request to view details

### Processing a Request

1. Open request details
2. Review client info, services requested, and any notes/photos
3. Update status as needed:
   - Mark as **Pending** while reviewing
   - Mark as **Accepted** when approved
   - Mark as **Rejected** if declining
4. Contact client if more info is needed

### Converting a Request to a Job

1. Open request details
2. Tap **Convert to Job**
3. Review pre-filled job details (client, services, site)
4. Adjust schedule, pricing, and assignments as needed
5. Tap **Save**
6. Request status automatically changes to **Converted**

### Referral Tracking (New Client Enquiries)

When creating a New Client Enquiry, record how they found you:
- Google Search
- Facebook/Instagram
- Friend/Family Referral
- Nextdoor
- Yelp
- Flyer/Postcard
- Other

### Consumables Order Workflow

1. Create request with type **Consumables Order**
2. Select products from catalog
3. Choose delivery method:
   - Client Pickup
   - Deliver on Next Visit
   - Ship to Client
   - Urgent Delivery
4. Process and fulfill order
5. Mark as complete when delivered

## Time Tracking

### Clocking In to a Job

1. Open the visit you're working on
2. Find the **Time Tracking** card
3. Tap **Clock In**
4. GPS will verify you're at the job site (if geofencing enabled)
5. Timer starts counting

**Note**: You can only be clocked into one job at a time.

### Clocking Out

1. Open the visit you're clocked into
2. Tap **Clock Out** in the Time Tracking card
3. If this is the last visit, you'll be prompted to close the job

### Automatic Clock In/Out (GPS)

If the site has GPS set to **Automatic**:
1. When you arrive at the job site, app auto-clocks you in
2. When you leave the site, app auto-clocks you out

If set to **Reminder**:
1. App prompts you to confirm clock in/out

### Viewing Your Timesheet

1. Go to **Timesheet** tab
2. Navigate weeks using arrows
3. Tap a day to expand and see time entries
4. View weekly totals at the bottom

## Visit Management

### Completing Forms During a Visit

1. Open the visit
2. Scroll to **Forms** section
3. Tap on a form template
4. Fill in all required fields
5. Add photos if needed
6. Tap **Submit**

### Adding Notes to a Visit

1. Open the visit
2. Scroll to **Notes** section
3. Tap **Add Note**
4. Enter your note text
5. Attach photos if needed
6. Tap **Save**

### Viewing Items to Bring

1. Open the visit
2. Scroll to **Items to Bring** section
3. Check off items as you pack them
4. Products assigned to the visit appear here

## Calendar & Scheduling

### Viewing Your Schedule

1. Go to **Calendar** tab
2. Tap on any date to see jobs for that day
3. Tap a job to view details

### Optimizing Your Route

1. Go to **Calendar** tab
2. Tap **Route** button in header
3. View optimized job order on map
4. Tap **Navigate** to open in maps app

### Viewing Staff Schedules (Managers)

1. Go to **More** tab
2. Tap **Staff Scheduler**
3. Toggle between Daily/Weekly/Monthly views
4. See all staff assignments and unassigned visits

## Client Communication

### Sending Job Confirmation

1. Open job details
2. Tap **Actions** menu
3. Select **Send Confirmation**
4. Review message preview
5. Tap **Send**

### Sending Quote to Client

1. Open quote details
2. Tap **Send to Client**
3. Client receives email with quote link

---

## Invoicing

### Creating an Invoice

**From a Job:**
1. Open job details
2. Scroll to Job Total
3. Tap **Generate Invoice**

**Standalone:**
1. Go to **More** → **Invoices**
2. Tap **+** to create new
3. Select client
4. Add line items
5. Tap **Save**

### Sending an Invoice

1. Open invoice details
2. Tap **Send to Client**
3. Client receives email with payment link

---

## Team Management (Managers/Admins)

### Adding a Team Member

1. Go to **More** → **Team Members**
2. Tap **Add Member**
3. Enter email, name, phone
4. Select role (Admin, Manager, Employee, Contractor)
5. Tap **Save**
6. Invitation email is sent to new member

### Assigning Staff to Visits

1. Open visit details
2. Scroll to **Assigned Staff** section
3. Tap **Assign**
4. Select team member
5. Tap **Confirm**

---

## Settings & Configuration

### Setting Your Profile

1. Go to **More** → **Profile**
2. Tap **Select your profile**
3. Choose your name from the team list
4. Your schedule and assignments will now be personalized

### Enabling Biometric Unlock

1. Go to **More** → **Profile**
2. Scroll to **Security** section
3. Toggle **Use Face ID/Touch ID**
4. Authenticate to confirm

### Updating Company Details

1. Go to **More** → **Company Details**
2. Edit company info, timezone, business hours
3. Tap **Save**

### Managing Products & Services

1. Go to **More** → **Products & Services**
2. Tap **+** to add new item
3. Enter name, description, price
4. Select type (Product or Service)
5. Tap **Save**
